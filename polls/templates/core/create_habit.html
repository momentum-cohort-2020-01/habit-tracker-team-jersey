Create a Habit

Here are a few tips:

'Habit Name' is whatever you want to call your new habit.

'Action' refers to the simple verb in the present tense. 'Eat' or 'Run', etc.

'Goal' is a number: how many instances of the action you want to acheive in a given timeframe.

'Unit' is a unit of measurement, in the singular. For istance, 'mile' or 'apple'.

And the 'Timeframe' is something like 'day', 'week', 'month', etc.

Go ahead and give it a try!
